Other Pertinent Information

Steve Wozniak’s first creation was a device called the “Blue Box” which was used to hack into phone networks to avoid paying for long distance phone calls,
which he would go onto sell to students that attended his University.

Additionally, he credits the movie “Star Trek” as the inspiration for the first Macintosh Personal Computer.

Steve Wozniak is still on the Apple payroll, bringing in $50 a week.
